Dorcas Muthoni

    Who is Dorcas Muthoni?


    Dorcas Muthoni is the founder and Chief Exceutive Officer of Openworld Ltd which is a specialist computer software company in Kenya. She founded this company when she was just 24 years old! Her startup story includes starting in university with only 5 other women engineers to build networks.


    Obstacles she overcame


    Dorcas mentioned in a interview with Reuters that she found she had to prove herself in her early years. She was met with skepticism that she could be successful in IT as a woman. A comment that she recalled was "Are you sure this is going to work out? I mean why don't you do something else, like leasing event furniture?" She also had to convince people that her home-grown brand could be as good as big brand like Microsoft which was difficult to do.


    Her impact:


    In 2004, Dorcas founded an organisation called AfChix which has the primary aim of building initiative for women in the region. She has also started a mentorship programme in high schools and involves bringing girls from different schools together and empowering them with access to ask questions to female web developers, engineers and build mentor-mentee relationships.
