Martha Lane Fox

    Who is Martha Lane Fox?

    Martha Lane Fox was named the most influential woman in digital technology in the last 25 years in 2019 at the Dadi Awards. Martha was the co-founder of Lastminute.com in the 1990s which grew favourably among the public. She was also the youngest female member of the House of Lords! She has an incredible career such as being on the Marks and Spencer and Channel 4 boards. Currently she is a chancellor at the Open University and sits on the board of Twitter and Chanel.


    Technology wisdom

    Martha remains passionate about technology and currently believes that technology should improve people's lives and tackle the climate crisis. Lady Lane Fox has stated that over the past decade it is clear that capitalism is not sustainable to keep without a cost.


    Doteveryone.

    Lady Lane Fox has developed Doteveryone to include responsible technology that works for everyone. This includes building better software and thinking about the environmental impact when building the software.
