Grace Beverley

    Who is Grace Beverley?

    Grace Beverley is a social media influencer and youtuber formerly known as FitGraceUk. She has founded 2 businesses known as B_ND and TALA while studying at Oxford University. Both TALA and B_ND focus on high-performance women's gymwear and gym equipment that supports ethical practice by being sustainable and affordable to the average shopper.Her brand TALA is made from 92% used materials such as plastic bottles and factory trimmings. Her businesses including SHREDDY her workout app have yielded an 8 million pound turnover last year. SHREDDY has seen 52k monthly active users since its launch in October 2019! Grace has won 'Young Entrepreneur of the Year in 2019 at the NatWest Great British Entrepreneur Awards.'



    What challenges did she face?

    After she graduated from Oxford, she moved her team to an office and has said that she often felt that she should show up with a suit to play the part. Grace has also spoke about dealing with imposter syndrome whilst she went through this change. Grace has also spoken up about the prejudice that is assoicated with being an social media influencer. She said that in external meetings it can be quite obvious that the people in the meetings have a prejudged opinion about her and may assume that she is just the 'face' of the brand rather than the owner.

    How did she overcome these obstacles?

    Grace has stopped worrying about how she is perceived in a meeting. She has said "let them have their preconceptions about me, I'm just going to get on with my job". Grace has also let go of the notion that to be seen seriously and professionally, you have to wear suits.


    Advice for another potential female entrepreneur?

    "I think it's really cruical to find people who believe in you and what your doing."
