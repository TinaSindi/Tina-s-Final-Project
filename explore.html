The explore page focuses on ambitious, pioneering female entrepreneurs who have sucessfully set up flourishing start-ups, companies and businesses. Here is our spotlight compilation of our top 6 women that we will focus on this week. You will be able to find out more about their businesses, growth, how they overcame disadvantages within the industry such as prejudice and more! Keep on reading to find out more!

Grace Beverley


Founder and Director at TALA, SHREDDY AND B_ND. Centres on ethical and sustainable products in the gymwear industry.

Reshma Saujani


Founder and CEO of Girls Who Code. Girls Who Code is an international non-profit organisation dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology and programming.

Cher Wang


Founder and CEO of the HTC computer manufacturing company. Regarded as one of the most successful female entrepreneurs in Taiwan in the technology sector.

Martha Lane Fox


British businesswoman who co-founded lastminute.com during the early 2000s. In 2013, she became the youngest female in the House of Lords!

Dorcas Muthoni


CEO and founder of Openworld Ltd in Kenya which she established when she was just 24 years old. Openworld is a e-Government and business software firm.

Huda Kattan


Beauty blogger, social media influencer-turned-entrepreneur built a $1.2 billion beauty brand- Huda Beauty. Number 36 on America's Self-Made Women, 2019.
